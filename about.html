About Us

Over twenty years of experience in facilities management, engineering, compliance and technical asset reporting.

Who We Are

CJ Consultancy Services Ltd provides independent mechanical, electrical, refrigeration and compliance reporting for facilities management providers across the UK. Our work ensures clarity, accuracy and confidence when taking responsibility for new or existing maintenance contracts.

Our Background

With over two decades of experience in the facilities management sector and engineering disciplines, we understand the risks associated with accepting responsibility for assets that may not have been maintained correctly or may be approaching the end of their indicative lifecycle.

Our reporting helps mitigate these risks by providing a clear, independent assessment of asset condition, operational status, compliance alignment and lifecycle position. This allows maintenance providers to make informed decisions, advise clients accurately and maintain business continuity.

What We Cover

We work across a wide range of building types including:

  • Commercial offices
  • Retail environments
  • Laboratories and specialist facilities
  • Industrial buildings
  • Data centres and critical environments
  • Modern complexes and repurposed historical buildings

Our Commitment

At CJ Consultancy Services Ltd, we pride ourselves on delivering precise, independent reporting that supports compliance, safety, operational efficiency and long‑term asset planning. Our goal is to provide the insight you need to operate with confidence.
