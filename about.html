About Us

CJ Consultancy Services Ltd is a specialist provider of mechanical and electrical compliance, technical inspection, lifecycle analysis, and asset condition reporting. We support commercial, industrial, public‑sector, and specialist environments with clear, actionable engineering insight that strengthens operational reliability, compliance assurance, and long‑term asset performance.

Our work is built on a simple principle: Your Vision, Our Expertise. We translate complex engineering requirements into precise, practical guidance that enables organisations to make informed decisions about safety, compliance, maintenance strategy, and capital investment.

Our Approach

We combine deep technical knowledge with rigorous inspection methodology to deliver reporting that is accurate, transparent, and aligned with real‑world operational needs. Every assessment, verification, and analysis is carried out with a focus on clarity, traceability, and long‑term value for the client.

Whether we are verifying critical assets, analysing lifecycle costs, or developing maintenance strategies, our goal is to provide engineering intelligence that supports safe operation, regulatory compliance, and efficient asset management.
