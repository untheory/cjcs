About CJ Consultancy Services Ltd

CJ Consultancy Services Ltd provides specialist mechanical, refrigeration, and electrical asset condition reporting, compliance verification, lifecycle analysis, and technical inspection services across commercial, industrial, and specialist environments.

We work closely with facilities maintenance providers on newly awarded contracts to assess the true condition of inherited assets. This mitigates risk by identifying equipment that may have been poorly maintained, incorrectly serviced, or nearing end‑of‑life. Our reporting ensures that maintenance providers understand the operational and business risks associated with the assets they are taking responsibility for.

Our services are equally valuable for existing maintenance contracts. By identifying indicative lifecycle stages, we help organisations advise clients on business continuity, future planning, and long‑term asset strategy.

Our Background

With over thirty years of experience in the Facilities Management sector — including electrical engineering, manufacturing engineering, and complex building systems — CJ Consultancy Services Ltd brings deep technical insight and practical industry knowledge to every project.

We work across all building types, from historical repurposed structures to modern purpose‑built complexes, delivering clear, accurate, and actionable engineering guidance.
