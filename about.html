About CJ Consultancy Services Ltd

Professional engineering compliance, inspection, and advisory services led by Clint Jefferys.

Company Overview

CJ Consultancy Services Ltd provides specialist mechanical and electrical compliance, technical inspection, and advisory services across commercial, industrial, and specialist environments. The company is built on high‑integrity reporting, legislative alignment, and practical engineering guidance designed for real‑world decision‑making.

Operating from Suffolk, UK, CJCS supports clients who require clarity, accuracy, and structured reporting that integrates seamlessly into asset strategies, maintenance planning, and compliance frameworks.

About the Managing Director

Clint Jefferys is the Managing Director of CJ Consultancy Services Ltd and an independent theoretical physics researcher. With over thirty years of professional experience in mechanical and electrical compliance, Clint has delivered high‑integrity inspection, reporting, and advisory services across a wide range of commercial, industrial, and specialist engineering environments.

His background includes:

  • Mechanical and electrical legislation & compliance
  • Technical inspection and defect classification
  • Asset condition reporting and life‑cycle profiling
  • Maintenance strategy development and optimisation
  • Independent review of third‑party reports
  • Specialist advisory support for complex environments

Clint is based in Hundon, Sudbury, Suffolk, United Kingdom.

Professional Values

CJCS operates with a clear set of professional values:

  • High‑integrity reporting
  • Legislative and standards alignment
  • Structured, practical outputs
  • Clear communication for decision‑makers
  • Traceability and auditability
  • Commitment to engineering clarity over complexity
