How to Begin an Engagement

To ensure clarity from the outset, enquiries should ideally include:

  • A brief description of the site, asset, or environment
  • The type of compliance or inspection required
  • Any existing reports or documentation (if applicable)
  • Preferred timelines or deadlines
  • Any specific legislative or standards‑based requirements

This information allows CJCS to provide a structured response, define scope, and outline expected deliverables.

Reporting Standards

All reports produced by CJ Consultancy Services Ltd follow a consistent structure:

  • Clear defect classification and prioritisation
  • Legislative and standards alignment
  • Traceable recommendations and remedial guidance
  • Asset‑focused insights for decision‑makers
  • Integration with maintenance and life‑cycle strategies

Professional Conduct

CJCS operates with high‑integrity engineering values:

  • Accuracy and clarity in all reporting
  • Independence and objectivity
  • Compliance with relevant legislation and standards
  • Confidential handling of client information
  • Commitment to practical, implementable guidance
