Our Professional Services

CJ Consultancy Services Ltd delivers specialist mechanical and electrical compliance, technical inspection, and engineering advisory services across commercial, industrial, and specialist environments.

Mechanical & Electrical Compliance

Ensuring full alignment with statutory requirements, industry standards, and best‑practice engineering protocols. Includes verification, inspection, and compliance reporting.

Asset Condition Reporting

Independent assessment of mechanical and electrical asset condition, degradation, operational status, and risk exposure. Supports maintenance planning and lifecycle decisions.

Asset Verification

Detailed verification of installed systems, equipment identity, configuration, and operational readiness. Ideal for commissioning, handover, and compliance audits.

Life Cycle Analysis & Costing

Engineering‑grade lifecycle modelling, cost forecasting, and replacement strategy development for long‑term asset management.

Business‑Focused Maintenance Reporting

Maintenance reporting aligned with operational priorities, risk tolerance, and business objectives. Supports strategic decision‑making and budget optimisation.

Business‑Focused Maintenance Scheduling

Development of maintenance schedules that balance compliance, reliability, and cost efficiency. Includes PPM planning and operational alignment.

Condition‑Based Maintenance

Reporting and scheduling based on real‑world asset condition, operational data, and degradation profiles. Reduces unnecessary maintenance and improves reliability.

Maintenance Gap Analysis

Identification of missing, incomplete, or misaligned maintenance activities. Provides actionable recommendations to restore compliance and operational integrity.

Functional Design Verification

Independent verification of design intent, functional requirements, and engineering specifications. Ensures systems meet operational and compliance expectations.

Design & Obsolescence Specifications

Creation of engineering specifications for new installations, upgrades, and obsolescence replacement programmes.

PPM Planners & Log Books

Development of Planned Preventative Maintenance planners, log books, and operational documentation for compliance and audit readiness.

Auditing & Technical Services

Independent auditing, technical inspection, and engineering advisory services across mechanical and electrical domains.
