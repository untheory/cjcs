Our Services

CJ Consultancy Services Ltd provides a comprehensive suite of mechanical and electrical engineering services designed to support safe operation, regulatory compliance, and long‑term asset performance.

Professional Services

Asset Condition Reporting
Detailed inspection and assessment of mechanical and electrical assets to determine operational health, risk level, and required interventions.

Asset Verification
Full validation of installed equipment against design specifications, compliance requirements, and operational expectations.

Mechanical and Electrical Compliance
Ensuring all systems meet statutory, regulatory, and industry standards through structured compliance audits and reporting.

Life Cycle Analysis
Evaluation of asset performance over its operational lifespan to optimise replacement planning and reduce long‑term costs.

Life Cycle Costing
Financial modelling of asset costs from installation to end‑of‑life, supporting budget planning and strategic investment decisions.

Business Focused Maintenance Reporting
Clear, actionable maintenance reports aligned with business priorities, operational risk, and performance objectives.

Business Focused Maintenance Scheduling
Strategic scheduling of maintenance activities to minimise downtime and maximise operational efficiency.

Condition Based Maintenance Reporting
Real‑time and periodic reporting based on asset condition data, enabling predictive maintenance strategies.

Condition Based Maintenance Scheduling
Maintenance planning driven by actual asset condition rather than fixed intervals, reducing unnecessary work and cost.

Maintenance Gap Analysis Reporting
Identification of missing, overdue, or insufficient maintenance activities to restore full compliance and operational safety.

Functional Design Verification
Technical verification that systems and installations meet functional design intent and operational requirements.

Design Specifications
Creation of detailed mechanical and electrical design specifications for new installations, upgrades, and refurbishments.

Obsolescence Specifications
Assessment and documentation of obsolete or end‑of‑life equipment, including upgrade pathways and risk mitigation.

PPM Planners
Development of Planned Preventative Maintenance schedules tailored to asset type, usage, and compliance requirements.

Log Books
Creation and management of asset log books for compliance, maintenance tracking, and operational transparency.

Auditing
Independent auditing of mechanical and electrical systems, maintenance regimes, and compliance documentation.

Technical Services
Specialist engineering support, consultancy, and problem‑solving across complex mechanical and electrical environments.
