Detailed Services Overview

Structured, high‑integrity engineering and compliance support across mechanical and electrical domains.

Mechanical Compliance & Inspection

Mechanical compliance services focus on asset integrity, risk‑based inspection planning, and alignment with relevant legislation and standards. Typical engagements include:

  • Plant and equipment condition assessments
  • Risk‑based inspection schedules and prioritisation
  • Defect identification, classification, and remedial guidance
  • Life‑cycle and replacement profiling for critical assets

Electrical Compliance & Technical Reporting

Electrical compliance services provide structured reporting, defect classification, and practical remedial strategies. Typical engagements include:

  • Compliance inspections and structured defect registers
  • Review of third‑party test results and reporting quality
  • Remedial prioritisation and implementation guidance
  • Integration of electrical findings into wider asset strategies

Asset Reporting, Maintenance & Advisory

Asset reporting and advisory services are built for decision‑makers who need clarity, not noise. Typical engagements include:

  • Asset condition reporting with clear, actionable grading
  • Maintenance scheduling and optimisation recommendations
  • Independent review of existing reports and maintenance plans
  • Technical advisory support for complex or specialist environments

Engagement Model

Engagements are scoped with clear deliverables, reporting standards, and alignment to relevant legislation and best‑practice guidance. Each project is structured to:

  • Define scope, assets, and reporting expectations upfront
  • Provide structured, legible outputs for decision‑makers
  • Integrate findings into practical, implementable strategies
  • Maintain traceability and auditability of recommendations
